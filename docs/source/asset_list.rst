==========
Asset List
==========

In investing, it is often necessary to compare the performance of different types of assets, stock indices,
currencies, and portfolios. For the convenience of such comparisons, *okama* library has the **AssetList class**.

AssetList includes several methods to analyze and compare historical return, various risk metrics of the assets (and indexes).
